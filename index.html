Our Story

Born from Passion,
Crafted for Distinction

Iphie's Lux Scents began with a single belief — that every person deserves a fragrance that tells their story. Founded in the heart of Nigeria, we source the finest raw materials from across the globe, blending tradition with contemporary artistry.

From the smoky depths of oud to the delicate whisper of jasmine at dawn, each bottle we offer is a chapter waiting to be worn. We don't just sell perfume — we curate experiences that linger in rooms long after you've left.

— Iphie
